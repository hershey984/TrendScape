Fashion encompasses various types and styles to cater to diverse tastes and occasions. These types include high fashion (haute couture), ready-to-wear, streetwear, casual wear, athletic wear, business and professional wear, ethnic and cultural fashion, vintage and retro fashion, bohemian fashion, sustainable and eco-friendly fashion, gender-neutral fashion, formal and evening wear, lingerie and intimate apparel, maternity and plus-size fashion, beach and swimwear, and wedding and bridal fashion. Each type of fashion serves specific purposes and reflects the creativity and preferences of designers and consumers. Fashion is an ever-evolving industry with something for everyone.
